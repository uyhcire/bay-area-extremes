Entry № 01 — Housing cost

The Costliest Big Metroin America

Among the 20 largest U.S. Combined Statistical Areas, the San Jose–San Francisco–Oakland CSA has the highest typical home value and the most extreme price-to-rent ratio in the country — despite ranking only 5th by population.

How it was found

Zillow has no CSA rows — so we rolled them up.

Zillow publishes ZHVI/ZORI at the CBSA (metro) level only. The CSA view is a join: Census county→CBSA→CSA crosswalk, county population to rank and weight, and Zillow's county files for the levels.

claude code — bay-area-extremes

· weight + size: Census county pop, 2023 vintage
· levels: Zillow County_zhvi / County_zori (Apr 2026)
